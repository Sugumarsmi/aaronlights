Custom LED Solutions for Retailers,Brands and Manufacturers

You may have different needs and specifications for your lighting solutions, leave it to us we can design and deliver around your needs &  specifications within your budget.

With in-house experts in the area of Optoelectronic,Thermal,Mechanical,Electrical & Design engineering we have the capability to deliver according to your specific needs

Aaron addresses each project with proper optical analysis and has in house tools to provide the most optimum solution for our customers.

We have a full fledged independent Electronics Design Centre which manages the design of electronic drivers for the LED's. The team designs led engines based on the product requirements and matches the drivers to the led configuration .

CONCEPT & DESIGN SERVICE

We believe in a structured process to define the product and project requirement of customers.

OPTICAL DESIGN SERVICE

We choose LED's based on the product application and select the right lens or reflector solution for our products.

THERMAL CHARACTERIZATION

Aaron focus a lot on the Thermal performance of the products we manufacture. We use scientific calculations and tools to arrive at the correct heatsink design to maintain LED junction temperatures within the designed limits for the required longevity of the product.

ELECTRONICS MFG & SERVICES

With careful component selection and design for manufacturability Aaron takes care that the life of the drivers are long enough to take advantage of the life of LEDs.Aaron takes care of best electrical standards related to power factor, total harmonic destertion, EM & EMC radiations.

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING SERVICES

Aaron has the expertise to convert the design into production with its knowledge of materails, aluminium die casting, Aluminium extrusion, Plastic Moulding etc. It has the expertise to design enclosures and gaskets for IP rated products. With the help of in house expertise and external consulting experts in Mechanical Engineering Aaron provides a completely scientific approach towards the Mechanical design of fixtures.

FULL LUMINAIRE DEVELOPMENT

With expertise in all subsegments Aaron is in a position to deliver complete luminaire solutions to its customers.
